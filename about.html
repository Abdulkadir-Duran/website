Introduction

DSiC youth voluntary organization is a Djiboutian Students in China non-governmental, non-profit making, independent of tribalism and discrimination. It was founded in June of 2017 to help the different parts of Djiboutian students for several below things our country mate faced many hard times inherited from the lack of organization which was left several destructive impacts such as lack of education.

We know many of us to face a challenge in China. I can not enumerate all the challenges over here. However, the frequent challenge is as follows. DSiC is here to advocate aware the new students, help each other among students (Students who went back home and graduate the process of notarizing documents, legalize and authenticate ( It needs someone who will deal on the behalf of student who already back his home), from the problem of Renewal Passport, from all kind of issue related Djiboutian Students in china.

This organization was formed for solving the above problems and increasing knowledge base of the Djiboutian Student in China (New Students can consult their senior about the information they need from China), also improve their skills and to teach how to learn better the Life of China.

Vision

DSiC strives to reduce the risks/problems of the Djiboutian Students in China Such, by helping each other, Networking , Social Skills, Professional experience, Personality development, Leadership Skills, Alumni Networking, Organization and Management Skills and Friendships and Fun to enhance social education.

Objectives

Mobilize and encourage community in order to enhance the development and education.

Raise literacy rate through providing non-formal education women, children, and sports.

Enhance youth knowledge on the socio-economic development.

Raise health awareness toward the community to prevent FGM and HIV/AIDS.

Strategy of the organization

SOPAD organization strategies consist of main approaches and are:

  1. Encouraging membership contribution and local contribution.
  2. Carry out researches and studies on development problems concerning media, sports, health awareness and education particularly in this region.
  3. Submit project international and plan to the fund raising agencies and international donors.
  4. To have contact with the target people or communities and activate the role of community participation and their environment.
